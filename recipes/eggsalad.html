Egg Salad

ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/3C light mayonaisse
  • 1Tbs dijon mustard
  • 1/4C finely dices onion or celery
  • Cholula hot sauce or your favorite brand
  • chives or scallions for garnish

Directions

  1. roughly chop eggs and add to a medium bowl
  2. add celery and/or onions
  3. add dijon
  4. fold in mayo
  5. add hot sauce to taste
  6. garnish with scallions/chives
